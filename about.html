I'm a Yorkshire-based creative working in marketing, communications, and design for the cultural sector.

With almost five years of experience, my work sits at the intersection of campaign delivery, content production, and visual design. I move between strategic thinking and hands-on making, depending on what a project needs.

Currently I work as Marketing Manager at the National Science and Media Museum in Bradford, where I run campaigns for exhibitions, gallery launches and major events. I also work freelance as Campaign Coordinator for Bradford Museums and Galleries, which brought me into projects like the Jameel Prize exhibition during Bradford 2025 UK City of Culture.

Before museums, I worked at Leeds Arts University running campaigns like the End of Year Show and Art Market. I studied Creative Advertising at the same university, which gave me a foundation in concept development, copywriting, and visual communication.
