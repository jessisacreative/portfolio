Work About

I'm a Yorkshire-based creative working in marketing, communications, and design for the cultural sector.

With almost five years of experience, my work sits at the intersection of campaign delivery, content production, and visual design. I move between strategic thinking and hands-on making, depending on what a project needs.

Currently I work as Marketing Manager at the National Science and Media Museum in Bradford (0.5 FTE job share), where I run campaigns for exhibitions, gallery launches and major events. I also work freelance as Campaign Coordinator for Bradford Museums and Galleries, which brought me into projects like the Jameel Prize exhibition during Bradford 2025 City of Culture.

Before museums, I worked at Leeds Arts University running campaigns like the End of Year Show and Art Market, and before that I was at Mediaworks UK Ltd creating social content for global FMCG brands. My background covers in-house roles, agency work, and freelance projects, which means I'm comfortable adapting to different scales, timelines and ways of working.

I'm interested in how cultural organisations connect with audiences, particularly around accessibility and reaching people who don't usually engage with museums and galleries. A lot of my work has been about translating complex or ambitious projects into campaigns that feel relevant and welcoming.
