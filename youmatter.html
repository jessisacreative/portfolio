YOU:MATTER

National Science and Media Museum, 2025

Client
National Science and Media Museum
Partners
Bradford 2025, Marshmallow Laser Feast
Role
Marketing Manager, Campaign Lead
Year
2025

YOU:MATTER is a spectacular immersive installation by experiential artist collective Marshmallow Laser Feast for Bradford 2025 UK City of Culture. The experience invites visitors to explore their connection to the universe, discovering how everything from the stars to each living cell is woven together.

I led the marketing and communications campaign, working closely with Bradford 2025 and the museum's exhibitions team. This was a joined-up campaign requiring careful coordination between multiple partners. I directed the creative content strategy, coordinating photo and video shoots to capture the immersive nature of the experience. Working with external agencies, I managed asset production while ensuring messaging remained accessible for diverse audiences.
