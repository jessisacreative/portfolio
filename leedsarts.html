Project 07

University Branding
& Marketing

Leeds Arts University, 2023–2024

Client
Leeds Arts University
Role
Marketing & Design Officer
Scope
Brand Rollout, Print Design, Event Coordination
Year
2023–2024

Following Leeds Arts University's website redesign, I led the rollout of new branding across the university. This included updating brand guidelines and redesigning core marketing materials to align with the refreshed visual identity.

I managed the application of the new branding across all university marketing materials: prospectuses, postcards, event assets, promotional materials for open days, graduations, and events. This wasn't just about applying a logo. It was about ensuring consistency, quality, and appropriate use of the new identity across everything we produced.

Part of my role involved developing student-led design competitions for merchandise. I briefed students, selected winners, and ensured their designs were vectorised correctly before organising print production. This created opportunities for students to see their work used by the university while keeping our merchandise feeling current and student-led.

I prepared artwork for print and managed production with external suppliers, balancing cost-effectiveness with quality and sustainability. I also coordinated the university's graduation ceremonies at the Royal Armouries in Leeds, working with the events team to ensure the ceremonies ran smoothly.
