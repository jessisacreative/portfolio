Reflections Film & Media, 2022–2023

Client

Sector

Role
Freelance Social Media Manager
Year
2022–2023

Reflections Film & Media is a production company specialising in early years and educational content. I worked with them as a freelance social media manager, handling their online presence and creating content that positioned them within the education and early years sectors.

I managed Reflections' social media presence, creating social video content and handling day-to-day channel management. This meant planning content calendars, creating engaging posts, and making sure their social channels reflected their expertise and professionalism.

I implemented new marketing and digital strategies, helping them think about how social media could support their wider business goals. Part of this involved designing event graphics and promotional materials (shell schemes, banners, flyers, digital media) for their presence at national industry events.

I visited these industry events to capture video and photography content for online coverage. This meant being on-site, documenting what was happening, and turning that into content that worked across their social channels.

Deliverables

  • Social media management (all platforms)
  • Social video content creation and editing
  • Channel management and content planning
  • Marketing and digital strategy
  • Event graphics and promotional materials
  • Shell schemes, banners, flyers, digital media
  • National industry event coverage
  • Video and photography content
