Project 05

Beyond the Frame

Leeds Arts University, 2024

Client
Leeds Arts University
Role
Marketing & Design Officer, Campaign Lead
Scope
Branding, Print, OOH, Exhibition Design
Year
2024

Beyond the Frame was Leeds Arts University's 2024 end of year exhibition, showcasing final year work from students across fifteen undergraduate courses including contemporary art, animation, illustration, graphic design, film, fashion, textiles, creative advertising, music, photography and writing.

I managed the full campaign from brief to delivery, starting in March 2024. This meant running the entire process, from briefing students through to launching the show three months later. I gave a briefing presentation to final year students eligible to submit ideas for the show branding, then attended the selection meeting with senior management to choose the winning design.

Once Katie Woodcock and Ben Pritchard-Swan were selected, I liaised with them to gather all their assets: fonts, logos, imagery, motion design. I prepared everything for distribution across digital and print formats. For the digital campaign, I created promotional GIFs as part of a partnership with Leeds List. I also organised the print and distribution of adverts across poster towers in student areas around Leeds.

On campus, I designed the printed exhibition guide, created large-scale window vinyl graphics, and developed signage across the building. This was about creating a unified visual identity that worked everywhere, from Instagram to the windows students walked past every day.
