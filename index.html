About

I'm a Yorkshire-based creative working in marketing, communications, and design for the cultural sector.

With almost five years of experience, my work sits at the intersection of campaign delivery, content production, and visual design. I move between strategic thinking and hands-on making, depending on what a project needs.

I'm interested in how cultural organisations connect with audiences, particularly around accessibility and reaching people who don't usually engage with museums and galleries. A lot of my work has been about translating complex or ambitious projects into campaigns that feel relevant and welcoming.

What I Do

I work across campaign planning, content creation, design, social media and press. Equally happy thinking strategically about how a campaign should work as I am coordinating a photoshoot, designing window graphics, or writing social copy.
