Project 04

The Great Leeds
Fungi Exhibition

Headrow House, Leeds, 2025

Curator
Studio Greenly
Venue
Headrow House, Leeds
Role
Exhibiting Artist, Event Photographer
Year
2025

The Great Leeds Fungi Exhibition was a group exhibition curated by Studio Greenly, a Leeds/Bradford based eco collective working in illustration and design. The exhibition celebrated the fascinating world of mushrooms and fungi through artistic depictions across ceramics, textiles, zines, paintings, prints, and photography.

I exhibited fungi-inspired photography exploring themes of decay, nature, and connection. My work looked at mushrooms not just as visual subjects but as symbols of transformation, decomposition, and the unseen networks that sustain ecosystems.

I also documented the exhibition as the event photographer throughout the weekend, capturing the artworks, the atmosphere, and the conversations that emerged. This meant being present across both days, photographing the works on display, the visitors engaging with them, and the live performances and music that brought the space to life.

The exhibition was a chance to show personal work alongside other artists who share a fascination with the mycelial world. It was a fun, creative weekend full of earthy textures, curious conversations, and shared love for the weird and wonderful world of fungi.
