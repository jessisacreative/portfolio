At Mediaworks, I worked on content strategy and production for global FMCG skincare brands, with a particular focus on TikTok and Instagram. This was high-volume content production in a fast-paced agency environment, creating social-first content for major international clients.

I developed strategic content for major UK FMCG clients, focusing on TikTok and Instagram. This meant creating video content, photography, and digital assets that worked natively on each platform, understanding trends, formats, and what would resonate with audiences.

I led TikTok content creation for the agency, including producing training resources for staff. This wasn't just about making content. It was about establishing best practices and upskilling the wider team so TikTok became a core part of our service offering.

I appeared in TikTok videos and produced social-first content based on trends and keyword research. This required staying on top of what was happening on the platform, moving quickly, and being comfortable on camera. I attended influencer events to capture behind-the-scenes coverage, bringing that content back to use across campaigns.

Deliverables

  • Content strategy and production (TikTok, Instagram)
  • Video content creation
  • Photography and digital asset production
  • TikTok content creation lead
  • Staff training resources for TikTok
  • On-camera talent (TikTok videos)
  • Social-first content based on trends
  • Influencer event coverage
  • TV and influencer PR campaign support
  • Augmented Reality filter creation
  • Platform launch support (global clients)
