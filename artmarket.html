Project 06

The Art Market

Leeds Arts University, 2024

Client
Leeds Arts University
Role
Marketing & Design Officer, Event Coordinator
Scope
Branding, Event Management, Financial Admin
Year
2024

The Art Market ran during the Beyond the Frame End of Year Show in June 2024. It was a student-run marketplace featuring 130+ students selling their work across prints, ceramics, textiles, zines, and illustration. This was a record-breaking year for sales.

I created the new branding and logos for the Art Market, giving it a distinct identity within the broader Beyond the Frame campaign. Two months before the event, I presented the brief to students, walking them through what was expected and how the market would work.

In the days leading up to the event, I checked students' stock and inventory lists, making sure everything was ready. I set up the displays for the merchandise that would be on sale throughout the week. During the event itself, I trained and managed teams of student ambassadors who were running the sales. This meant making sure they understood how to handle transactions, manage stock, and provide a good experience for buyers.

After the event, I handled all the financial distribution, gathering banking details from over 130 students and ensuring everyone received their earnings. Students had worked all year to create this work, and getting paid fairly and on time mattered.
